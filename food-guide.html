Tips for buying ingredients

When choosing fresh beef, you should choose uniform bright red, firm meat, uniform fat distribution like marble patterns;when choosing broccoli, you should choose a dark green color with firm flower balls, and the incision at the bottom stem should be fresh and moist.

How to handle ingredients

Thawing treatment of salmon/codfish: naturally thaw frozen fish or soak and thaw it with cold water to avoid high-temperature thawing and loose meat. Separate the main bone: cut the knife from the back of the fish to the abdomen, and stick to the main bone to make large pieces of fish; after removing the main bone, check whether there are residual thorns on the surface of the fish, and remove it directly.

Suggestions for storing ingredients
